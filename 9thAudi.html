Audi A4 features

1.The current-generation Audi A4 is approaching the end of its lifespan, and that becomes clearer once you look inside.
2.The touch screen responds quickly to inputs, the graphics are crisp, and the menus are easy to understand.
3.The base Audi A4 gets an EPA-rated 25 mpg in the city and 34 mpg on the highway, which is great for a luxury small car.
4.The 2022 Audi A4 and S4 both come in three trims: Premium, Premium Plus, and Prestige.

3.Audi Pre Sense Basic (rolls up windows and tightens seat belts if sensors detect an imminent collision)
